# ❓ Q&A Prep

> Expected questions & talking points

## Q: What does M365 Copilot cost vs. Copilot Chat?

```demo
Copilot Chat is included with M365 E3/E5 at no additional cost — it gives you general AI, web search, and file upload summarization. M365 Copilot is an add-on at ~€30/user/month (exact price depends on your agreement). The difference: M365 Copilot connects to your actual work data — emails, meetings, Teams transcripts, SharePoint files. For knowledge workers like engineers and managers handling 60-100 emails a day, that connection typically saves 1-2 hours daily and pays for itself in under a week.
```

## Q: Is our data secure? What about German data residency?

```demo
Yes. Microsoft 365 Copilot uses the same data residency, security, and compliance controls as your existing M365 environment. For German tenants, data is processed and stored in EU data centers. Microsoft Copilot is built on Azure OpenAI Service with enterprise-grade data protection — your prompts and responses are not used to train OpenAI's models. Your existing M365 compliance policies, DLP rules, and information barriers all apply to Copilot interactions.
```

## Q: Does Copilot integrate with SAP?

```demo
Yes — via Microsoft Graph connectors. SAP has a certified connector for M365 Copilot that brings SAP data (production orders, material master, procurement status) into Copilot's context. This means you can ask Copilot questions that combine your Outlook emails, Teams meetings, AND SAP production data in a single answer. The SAP connector is Generally Available. Implementation requires your IT team to configure the Graph connector — typically a 2-4 week project.
```

## Q: What's the ROI for manufacturing and engineering teams?

```demo
Microsoft's own research shows knowledge workers save 1.5-2 hours per day with Copilot. In manufacturing and engineering: engineers writing PPAP reports, 8D analyses, and meeting summaries save 30-45 minutes per document. Account managers preparing RFQ responses save 2-4 hours per proposal. Operations managers doing daily inbox triage save 45-90 minutes daily. At €30/user/month for a user saving 1 hour/day at €60/hour fully loaded cost, the ROI is 12:1 in the first month.
```

## Q: Copilot Chat vs. M365 Copilot — when does the upgrade make sense?

```demo
Copilot Chat is great for people who need general AI help — web research, drafting from scratch, uploading a file to summarize. The upgrade to M365 Copilot makes sense when the user wants Copilot to know their work: reading their emails, summarizing their meetings, searching their SharePoint files, understanding the context of their projects. For anyone who processes more than 30 emails a day or attends more than 3 meetings per week — the M365 Copilot upgrade pays for itself immediately.
```

## Q: Can Copilot work on local files?

```demo
Yes — since April 2026, Copilot can work with locally stored files on your device (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) when they're opened in the desktop app. You can also upload files directly to Copilot Chat for one-time analysis. For the best experience, OneDrive sync is recommended so files are also cloud-accessible — but it's no longer a hard requirement as of April 2026.
```
