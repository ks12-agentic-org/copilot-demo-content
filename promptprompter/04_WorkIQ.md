# 🧠 Work IQ / Day 1

> ⏱ 10 min

```demo
10 minutes. For new Copilot users who just got their license. Show them the most immediately valuable capability: understanding your own work context — inbox, calendar, cross-app connections. All grounded in your real M365 data — inbox, calendar, meetings, and files.
```

## Morning Inbox Catch-Up (0:20)

### Copilot Chat in M365 app → Work IQ → inbox grounded prompt.

```prompt
What's in my inbox that actually needs me this morning? Skip the newsletters. I care about anything from Contoso Ltd., Fabrikam GmbH, or from my team about open project issues. What requires a decision from me today, and what can wait until this afternoon?
```

## Calendar Briefing (0:22)

### M365 Copilot Chat → calendar + email context → paste prompt.

```prompt
Brief me on today's meetings. For each one: who's joining, what's the topic, and is there anything from my email or past meetings I should know going in? I have the Contoso quarterly review at 10am — is there anything outstanding from our last project sync I should prep?
```

## Cross-App: Contoso Project Status (0:24)

### M365 Copilot Chat → cross-app intelligence → grounded on email + meetings + files → concise status output.

```prompt
What's the current status on the Contoso digital transformation project? Pull from my emails, meetings, and any documents — I want: what's been agreed, what's still open, and what's blocking us. Then draft a 3-sentence status update I can paste into my weekly report.
```

## Find Action Items from Meetings (0:25)

### M365 Copilot Chat → cross-app → meeting transcripts + email search → draft follow-ups.

```prompt
What action items have been assigned to me in the last two weeks? Check my meeting transcripts and emails. Focus on anything related to the Contoso migration project or the Fabrikam contract negotiation — list them with owner and deadline. Then flag the 2 most overdue and draft a short follow-up message for each.
```

## Catch Up After Absence (0:26)

### M365 Copilot Chat → Work IQ → catchup mode, grounded on email + meetings.

```prompt
I was out sick for 3 days. What did I miss? Give me the highlights — key decisions made, anything about the Fabrikam contract renegotiation, any escalations on the Northwind Corp. delivery timeline, and any new requests from Contoso or Adventure Works.
```

## Find Everything About a Project (0:27)

### M365 Copilot Chat → cross-app search: SharePoint + email + Teams → unified account view. The "I didn't know it could search everywhere" moment.

```prompt
Find all files and emails about the Contoso account — check SharePoint, Teams, and last two weeks of email. Give me: the latest account brief, any open action items, and who owns the relationship. Then find everything from Fabrikam GmbH from the past month and tell me if there's anything I haven't responded to yet.
```

## Prepare for a High-Stakes Meeting (0:28)

### M365 Copilot Chat → meeting prep → grounded on email + files + past meetings. Outstanding wow moment: Copilot surfaces prior commitments you forgot about.

```prompt
I have a call with Contoso Ltd. procurement tomorrow about the Q3 contract renewal. Help me prepare: what's our current commercial position, what did we last agree to, what are the risks if we accept their proposed pricing terms, and what are the top 3 questions I should ask?
```

## Cross-App: Who Knows About This Customer? (0:28)

### M365 Copilot Chat → cross-org intelligence → search email + org knowledge → draft internal outreach. Shows Copilot as institutional memory.

```prompt
Who in our company has been in contact with Northwind Corp. before? Search my emails and shared documents. Give me a summary of what's been discussed, any existing relationship context, and any commitments made. Then draft a short internal Teams message to those people asking them to share their notes before I start the proposal process.
```

Focus on the Contoso deal, the Fabrikam onboarding project, and the Adventure Works proposal. Pull from my emails, meetings, and Teams messages. I'll use this as my Friday management update.

## Writing Help: Quick Context Switch (0:30)

### M365 Copilot Chat → write from context → grounded email draft.

```prompt
I need to write an update email to the VP of Sales about the Q2 pipeline status. Based on what you know from my emails and recent meetings, draft a 5-sentence executive summary for her: current pipeline total, biggest risk (flag Contoso if there are open blockers), what needs her support, and next week's priorities. Keep it crisp and action-oriented — executive level, no fluff.
```
