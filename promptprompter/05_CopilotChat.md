# 💬 Copilot Chat (Free with M365)

> ⏱ 5 min

```demo
5 minutes. For users who have M365 E3/E5 but no M365 Copilot license. They can access Copilot Chat at no additional cost. Show what they can do today — and when it makes sense to upgrade.
```

## Research: EV Cable Market Trends

```demo
Grounded web search — no license needed. Great first demo to show the power of Copilot Chat.
```

### copilot.microsoft.com → web search enabled → paste prompt. No Copilot license needed.

```prompt
What are the latest trends in high-voltage EV cable systems for 800V battery architectures in 2025-2026? I work for a cable manufacturer and want to understand where the market is heading — especially for fast charging infrastructure and battery management systems. Include key players and technology challenges.
```

## Draft from Scratch: Customer Email

```demo
General drafting — no org data needed. Works with Copilot Chat.
```

### copilot.microsoft.com → paste prompt. No M365 data access needed.

```prompt
Help me draft a professional email to our procurement contact at Fabrikam AG. We're responding to their RFQ for a new product system. Key points to include: we're interested in the project, we need 2 more weeks to complete technical feasibility (we may need to extend our current certification scope), we want to schedule a technical alignment call, and we're committed to their target SOP date. Tone: professional but warm, partnership-focused. Keep it under 200 words.
```

## Summarize Uploaded File

```demo
Upload a Word document to Copilot Chat and ask questions about it. No Copilot license — file upload works in chat.
```

### copilot.microsoft.com → attach file → paste prompt. Works without Copilot license.

```prompt
[Upload Q2_Sales_Pipeline.docx or your own pipeline document first]

Summarize this Q2 sales pipeline document for me. I need a 3-bullet executive summary: the total pipeline value, the highest-risk deal and why, and what needs to happen before end of Q2 to hit the Q3 target. Keep it sharp — I have 2 minutes before a board call.
```

## Brainstorm: RFQ Strategy

```demo
Use Copilot Chat as a thinking partner. No org data needed.
```

```prompt
I'm preparing an RFQ response for a new customer (Fabrikam AG). This is our first engagement with them. We have strong existing relationships with Contoso Ltd. and Northwind Corp. but are new to this customer. What are the 5 most important things to get right in this first RFQ response to build trust and win the business — from an established supplier's perspective?
```
