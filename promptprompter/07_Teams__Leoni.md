# 💬 Copilot in Teams

> ⏱ 8 min

```demo
8 minutes. Weekly engineering reviews, OEM alignment calls, plant operations meetings — all in Teams. Meeting intelligence makes every past conversation searchable and actionable.
```

## Meeting Recap — Missed Engineering Review (0:40–0:44)

```demo
Classic scenario: quality escalation pulled you into a crisis call while the key customer design review started. You joined 20 minutes late. Copilot catches you up instantly.
```

### In Teams meeting with transcript active → Copilot icon → ask this question.

```prompt
I had to jump on an urgent quality call and missed the first 20 minutes of the Contoso Ltd. engineering review. Catch me up — what did they discuss before I joined? What decisions were made, what action items were assigned to our team, and what open questions are we still waiting on from Contoso Ltd.?
```

## Q&A from Past Meeting (0:44–0:48)

```demo
The Fabrikam AG intro call was 2 weeks ago. You're now preparing the RFQ response and need to recall what was said about their technical requirements and timeline.
```

### Teams Copilot Chat → reference past meeting → ask Q&A from transcript.

```prompt
In our first intro call with Fabrikam AG two weeks ago, what did they say about their technical requirements for the product system? Specifically: did they mention the key performance requirements and their target SOP date? And was anything said about their preferred specifications or testing requirements? I need this to finalize our RFQ response.
```
