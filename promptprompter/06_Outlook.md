# 📧 Copilot in Outlook

> ⏱ 10 min

```demo
10 minutes. Engineers and managers handle 60-120 emails per day. Specs updates, OEM escalations, supplier issues, internal meeting prep. Copilot in Outlook cuts inbox time by 40-60%.
```

## Email Triage — Back from Customer Site (0:30–0:34)

```demo
3 days at a customer site. 60 unread emails. Classic engineering manager scenario.
```

```demo
💬 Say:
```

### Outlook → Copilot sidebar → paste prompt. No file needed.

```prompt
I've been at the Contoso Ltd. design review for 3 days. I have 60 emails waiting. Tell me: what's urgent and needs a response today, what's from Contoso Ltd. or Fabrikam AG that I should read first, is there anything about our key project specs or production quality issues at Contoso Plant B, and what can I safely ignore for now?
```

## Summarize Thread + Draft Reply (0:34–0:37)

```demo
Complex thread about a Contoso plant defect rate issue. 14 replies. Copilot reads it, catches you up, and drafts the management escalation reply.
```

### Open quality issue thread → Copilot summarize → draft reply.

```prompt
Summarize this email thread. I need to understand: what the quality issue at Contoso Plant B is exactly, what corrective actions have already been taken, and what's still open. Then draft a reply to the operations director — acknowledge the seriousness, confirm we've escalated to senior management, and request a status call tomorrow morning. Professional, direct tone. Include a proposed meeting time of 9am CET.
```

## Draft from Scratch — RFQ Follow-Up (0:37–0:40)

```demo
New business opportunity: Fabrikam AG RFQ. You need to respond by Friday and coordinate with 3 internal teams. Copilot drafts the coordinating email instantly.
```

### New email → Copilot Draft with Copilot → paste prompt. Optionally attach RFQ doc.

```prompt
I just received an RFQ from Fabrikam AG for a new product system — they want a response by this Friday. I need to coordinate with R&D on technical feasibility, with pricing on the commercial proposal, and with quality on certification scope. Draft an internal email to these three teams that: explains the opportunity (first engagement with Fabrikam AG, significant revenue potential), sets the Friday deadline clearly, assigns specific asks to each team, and requests a joint 30-minute call tomorrow at 14:00 to align before the response goes out.
```
